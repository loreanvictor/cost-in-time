Data Protection


this app stores all your data in your browser's local storage, and does not send it to anyone anywhere. of course your browser is able to send your data to someone but all mainstream browsers promise not to do that.

this app loads some scripts, fonts and styles from google and fontlibrary.org, so they might collect your IP address (although it is highly unlikely). use a VPN if you want to avoid that.
